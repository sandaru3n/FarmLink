# Rate Transporter - Quick Guide 🚀

## How to Use

### Step 1: Navigate to Orders
1. Open the **Distributor Dashboard**
2. Tap **"My Orders"** tab at the bottom
3. Select **"Farmer Orders"** tab

### Step 2: Find Delivered Order
1. Expand an order card (tap the dropdown)
2. Look for orders with **"DELIVERED"** status (green badge)
3. Scroll to the **Transporter Section** (purple card)

### Step 3: Rate the Transporter
1. Tap the **"Rate Transporter"** button
   - **Yellow/Amber gradient** if not rated yet
   - **Orange gradient** if already rated

### Step 4: Provide Rating
1. **Tap stars** to select rating (1-5 stars)
2. **Select categories** (optional):
   - Punctuality
   - Communication
   - Care of Goods
   - Professionalism
   - Reliability
   - Friendliness
3. **Add comments** (optional)
4. **Add feedback** (optional)
5. Tap **"Submit Rating"** or **"Update Rating"**

## Troubleshooting

### Button Not Appearing?
- ✓ Check if order is delivered
- ✓ Verify transporter was assigned
- ✓ Wait for status update

### Can't Submit?
- ⚠️ Must select at least 1 star
- ⚠️ Check internet connection
- ⚠️ Try closing and reopening dialog

### Want to Update Rating?
- ✓ Tap the orange "Update Rating" button
- ✓ Make your changes
- ✓ Tap "Update Rating" to save

## Example Flow

### First Time Rating:
```
1. Tap "Rate Transporter" (Yellow Button)
   ↓
2. Select 5 stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

```
1. Tap "Update Rating" (Orange Button)
   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓
5. ✅ Success! Rating updated
